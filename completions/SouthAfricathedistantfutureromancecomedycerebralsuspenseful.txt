In the distant future, South Africa is a peaceful country ruled by a monarchy. The king and queen have two daughters, Princess Rose and Princess Lily. Princess Lily is in love with a commoner named John, who works in the royal palace. Princess Rose is in love with a prince named William, who is heir to the throne of another country. When the king and queen find out about the relationships, they forbid the two couples from seeing each other.

The two couples must find a way to be together without getting caught. They must also find a way to stop the war that is brewing between their two countries. Along the way, they must deal with obstacles such as jungle animals, evil villains, and treacherous terrain.

Will the two couples find a way to be together? Will they be able to stop the war?
