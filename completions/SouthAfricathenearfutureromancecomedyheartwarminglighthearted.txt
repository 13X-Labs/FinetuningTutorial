In a future South Africa, two young people from different backgrounds fall in love against the odds.

Naledi is a black girl from a poor township who has managed to get a place at a prestigious university. She is studying hard to become a doctor.

Tumi is a rich white boy who has been brought up with everything he could ever want. He is studying business at the same university as Naledi.

The two of them meet by chance and sparks fly immediately. They start dating secretly, knowing that their families would never approve.

 gradually, they fall deeper in love, but the obstacles in their way seem insurmountable.

However, they are determined to fight for their love and, against all the odds, they succeed in finding happiness together.
