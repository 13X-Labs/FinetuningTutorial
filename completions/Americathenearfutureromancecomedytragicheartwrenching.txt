In a future America, relationships are no longer allowed. After years of watching the country tear itself apart over love, the government has decided to ban all forms of romance. But not everyone is willing to give up love so easily.

Jenn and Brian are two young people who desperately want to be together, but the law is standing in their way. With the help of their friends, they set out to change the system and bring love back to America.

The film follows their fight for love against all odds, and explores the meaning of love in a world that has forgotten it.
