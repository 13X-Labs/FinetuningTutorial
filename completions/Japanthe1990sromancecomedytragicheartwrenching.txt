Misako Takahashi is a beautiful young woman who has just been diagnosed with a terminal illness. She is given only a year to live, and so she decides to spend her last year enjoying life to the fullest. She moves to Tokyo and takes a job as a bar hostess, where she meets a handsome young man named Tatsuya Suzuki.

Despite their different backgrounds, the two fall in love and decide to spend Misako's last year together. However, as Misako's health begins to deteriorate, Tatsuya realizes that he may not be able to handle watching the woman he loves die. He tries to distance himself from her, but Misako is not ready to give up on their relationship.

As Misako's time runs out, the two must confront their feelings for each other and decide what they want their future to be.
