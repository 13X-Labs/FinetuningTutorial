In the land of America, during the Renaissance period, there was great tragedy and heartbreak. A young woman, who was betrothed to a wealthy man, was caught in an arranged marriage. However, she loved another man. A man who was not of her social class. A man who could never be hers.

The story follows the young woman as she tries to escape her fate and find true love. But, alas, it is not meant to be and the young woman must accept her fate. The story ends with the young woman marrying the man she does not love and living unhappily ever after.
