In the future, France is a magical place where anything is possible. seventeen-year-old Adele seems to have it all: she's beautiful, she's rich, she's popular, and she's about to marry the man of her dreams. But there's one catch: Adele is a witch, and her betrothed, Prince Charlemagne, is a vampire.

As their wedding day approaches, Adele and Charlemagne must overcome their differences and learn to work together if they want to have any chance of a happy ending. Along the way, they'll have to contend with meddling family members, a jealous ex-girlfriend, and a whole host of other obstacles.

But if they can stick together, they just might find that they were always meant to be together...
