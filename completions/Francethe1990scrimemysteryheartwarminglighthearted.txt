Patrice Lecomte, a young man in his early 20s, has just been released from prison after serving time for a crime he didn't commit. He is eager to start over and rebuild his life, but quickly realizes that the outside world is not as forgiving as he thought it would be. Desperate and alone, he turns to a life of crime to make ends meet.

Patrice quickly finds himself in over his head, as he is constantly pursued by the police and rival gangs. But he is determined to make it out alive and finally clear his name. With the help of his friends, he takes on the criminal underworld to find the real culprit and prove his innocence.

Full of twists and turns, this heartwarming crime mystery story is sure to keep you on the edge of your seat.
