In a future America, crime is at an all-time high. The police are overwhelmed and the public is terrified. Enter John Doe, a private investigator with a brilliant mind and a mysterious past. When a wealthy couple is murdered, Doe is hired to find the killers. But as he delves deeper into the case, he realizes that the murders are only the tip of the iceberg. With the help of his assistant, Jane Smith, Doe uncover a diabolical plot that could destroy the city – and the country – if it isn't stopped.

As the bodies start piling up, Doe and Smith race against the clock to find the killers and stop them before it's too late. With twists and turns at every turn, this is a crime & mystery film that will keep you guessing until the very end.
