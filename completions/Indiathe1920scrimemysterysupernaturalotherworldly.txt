In the 1920s, India is a land of mystery and magic, where the supernatural is an everyday occurrence. motoring through the country is a young woman named Lily, who is searching for her missing sister. Lily's sister, Rose, vanished while on a pilgrimage to a sacred site in the Himalayas, and Lily is convinced that she is still alive and being held captive by a powerful demon.

Lily enlists the help of a local shaman, who takes her on a journey into the spirit world in search of Rose. They travel to a dark and dangerous place where the demon resides, and Lily must use all her courage and strength to rescue her sister and defeat the demon.
