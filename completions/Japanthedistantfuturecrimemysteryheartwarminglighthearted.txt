In the future, Japan is a very different place. Crime is rampant, and the police are struggling to keep up. One officer, Detective Akiko, is determined to make a difference. She's a tough cop with a heart of gold, and she's not afraid to take on the toughest cases. Her latest case is the murder of a young woman. The victim's body was found in a dumpster, and she had been strangled. Detective Akiko is determined to find the killer and bring them to justice. She interviews the victim's friends and family, and she quickly learns that the victim was involved in a lot of shady business. She was a prostitute, and she was also involved in drug smuggling. Detective Akiko is able to track down the victim's last client, and she soon realizes that he is the killer. She arrests him and brings him to justice.
