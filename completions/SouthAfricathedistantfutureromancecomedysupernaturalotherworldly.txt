In the future, the world is a lot different than it is today. In South Africa, a group of friends with supernatural abilities live in a community that is hidden from the rest of the world. They use their powers to help people and make their lives better. One day, they meet a girl who is different from them. She is a human who has been gifted with supernatural powers. They help her to control her powers and use them for good. Over time, they fall in love with each other and the relationship is put to the test when the girl's powers start to fade. They must find a way to save her before it's too late.
