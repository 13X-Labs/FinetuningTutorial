In the future, France is a utopian society where the citizens are happy and content. However, there is one problem - the government is corrupt and the people are not allowed to fall in love. One day, a young woman named Juliette meets a handsome man named Romeo and they instantly fall in love. However, they must keep their relationship a secret from the government or they will be punished. As they try to find ways to be together, the government starts to suspect that something is going on between them. They are eventually caught and Romeo is sent to prison. Juliette is heartbroken and must find a way to free him. With the help of her friends, she comes up with a plan to overthrow the government and restore love to the people of France.
