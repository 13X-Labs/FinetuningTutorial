Maggie and Michael are young, in love, and about to get married. However, Maggie's father, a conservative senator, disapproves of their relationship and does everything he can to prevent the two from getting married. Meanwhile, Maggie and Michael are determined to find a way to be together, and with the help of their friends, they come up with a plan.

The film follows the young couple as they navigate their way through the challenges of their relationship, while also dealing with the interfering meddles of Maggie's father. Along the way, they learn important lessons about themselves, each other, and what it truly means to be in love. In the end, they prevail, and their love is stronger than ever.
