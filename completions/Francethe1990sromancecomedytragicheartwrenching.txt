Tragic Romance/Comedy

Set in France during the 1990s 

Our story follows Sarah, a young woman living in Paris with her boyfriend Francois. They are madly in love with each other and life couldn't be better. 

However, their idyllic life is turned upside down when Francois is diagnosed with a rare form of cancer. Sarah is heartbroken but vows to stand by her man and support him through his treatment. 

Francois bravely battles the disease but ultimately loses the fight. Sarah is left devastated but finds comfort in knowing that he is now at peace. 

The film ends with Sarah walking through the streets of Paris, remembering all the happy times she shared with Francois. She knows that although he is gone, he will always be with her in her heart.
