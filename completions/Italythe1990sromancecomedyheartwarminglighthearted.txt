In the 1990s, Angie (a young American woman) is working as an au pair in Italy. She meets and falls in love with Gino (a local Italian man). They have a whirlwind romance and get married. They are very happy together and have a baby girl. However, Angie's mother is not happy with the marriage and does not approve of Gino. She comes to visit and causes trouble for the young couple. Angie's mother leaves and Angie is left to deal with her own issues with Gino. They eventually work out their differences and stay together. They live happily ever after with their daughter.
