In the future, South Africa is a peaceful and prosperous country. However, the government is corrupt and the people are oppressed. One day, a young woman named Sizwe (Nomzamo Mbatha) decides to fight back against the government. She is joined by her friends, Mandla (Chiwetel Ejiofor) and Zodwa (Thandie Newton). Together, they start a revolution that changes the course of history.

The film follows their fight for freedom and justice, as well as their budding romance. Along the way, they face many challenges, but ultimately succeed in overthrowing the corrupt government and bringing peace and democracy to South Africa.
