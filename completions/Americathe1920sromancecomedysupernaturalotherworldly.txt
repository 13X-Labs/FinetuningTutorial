The Fitzgeralds are a wealthy family living in the Roaring Twenties. They are well-known for their lavish parties and their flamboyant lifestyle. However, behind the scenes, they are a family of witches and warlocks. One day, they receive an invitation to a party at a nearby estate. When they arrive, they realize that the party is actually a masked ball for a group of supernatural creatures. The Fitzgeralds are soon thrown into a world of magic and mystery, and they must use their powers to solve the mysteries of the Estate and save the creatures from a evil force that is trying to destroy them. Along the way, they fall in love with each other and discover more about their own powers.
