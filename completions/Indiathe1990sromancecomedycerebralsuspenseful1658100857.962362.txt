In India during the 1990s, a romance and comedy film set in a small town. A young woman, Maya, is in love with a man, Rishi, who is about to be married to another woman. However, when Maya's father dies, she is left with no choice but to marry Rishi. Rishi's family is against the marriage, but Maya is determined to make it work. With the help of her friends, she sets out to win over Rishi's family and prove that love can conquer all.
