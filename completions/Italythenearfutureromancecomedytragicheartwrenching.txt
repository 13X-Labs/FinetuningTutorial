In the near future, an American woman named Sarah travels to Italy for a much-needed vacation. She falls in love with the country and its people, and finds herself attracted to a handsome Italian man named Federico. They begin a whirlwind romance, and Sarah is soon head-over-heels in love. 

However, their happiness is short-lived. Sarah contracts a rare and incurable disease, and is given only a few months to live. Federico is devastated, but remains by her side throughout her illness. As Sarah's health deteriorates, they both come to terms with the fact that she will not survive.

In the end, Sarah dies in Federico's arms. It is a tragedy, but their love is true and beautiful, and will live on in memory.
