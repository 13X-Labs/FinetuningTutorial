In the near future, executive headhunter Kate Sinclair is in Italy searching for a CEO for a major corporation. While there, she meets Gabriele Rossi, a charming and handsome vineyard owner. They sparks fly immediately and they fall head over heels in love with each other.

However, their relationship is not without its challenges. Kate is a high-powered career woman used to being in control, while Gabriele is a laid-back Italian who loves nothing more than spending lazy days in the sun. They must learn to compromise with each other if their relationship is going to work.

But in the end, their love for each other prevails and they stay together, enjoying the best of both their worlds. They even get married and live happily ever after.
