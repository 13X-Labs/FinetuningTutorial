When Mallory moves to small town America she's expecting the worst. However, she soon discovers that the town is home to a host of supernatural creatures, and she quickly falls in love with the quirky and charming residents. While trying to come to terms with her new life, Mallory also has to deal with her meddling family, who are constantly trying to set her up with the perfect man. But when Mallory finally meets her match, she realizes that he may just be too good to be true.
