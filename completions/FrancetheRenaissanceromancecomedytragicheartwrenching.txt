In France during the Renaissance, a young woman named Gabrielle is tragically widowed. She is heartbroken and doesn't know how she will go on. But then she meets a handsome young man named Philippe. He is a kind and gentle soul, and he makes her feel alive again. They fall in love and are married. But then Philippe is killed in a hunting accident, and Gabrielle is once again left widowed. This time, she is broken beyond repair. She spends the rest of her days in mourning, never to love again.
