In the near future, in France, a young woman named Louise finds herself struggling to find love. She has been single for a while, and her friends are beginning to think that she will never find the right man. However, one day, Louise meets a man named Pierre, and she instantly falls in love with him. They begin dating, and everything seems to be going well. However, Pierre is hiding a secret from Louise: he is actually a time traveler from the future, and he has come back to prevent a future disaster from occurring. While Pierre is hesitant to tell Louise the truth, he eventually does, and she is understanding and supportive. Together, they use Pierre's knowledge of the future to help people in the present, and they fall even deeper in love in the process.
