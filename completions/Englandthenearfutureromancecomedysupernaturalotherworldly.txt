After the loss of her father, Clara seeks solace in her work as an astronomer. She discovers a comet that is on a collision course with Earth and sets out to warn the world. However, no one believes her and she is ridiculed by the scientific community. She is left feeling hopeless until she meets a mysterious man, who seems to know everything about her.

Clara is instantly drawn to the man, who tells her that he is a time traveler from the future. He has come to warn her about the comet and help her save the world. Together, they set out to track down the comet and stop it from destroying the Earth. Along the way, they fall in love with each other and their adventure becomes a race against time to save the world and their own relationship.
