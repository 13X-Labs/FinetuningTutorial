When American art student, Olivia, falls in love with a mysterious stranger she meets in Italy, she has no idea that he is actually a secret prince, living undercover as he prepares to take over the throne.

As they get to know each other, the two of them can't help but be drawn to each other, despite the fact that they come from two different worlds. However, when Olivia's true identity is revealed, the prince's disapproving parents do everything in their power to keep the two of them apart.

Will the prince and Olivia be able to overcome the odds and be together, or will they be forced to live apart forever?
