In Japan during the Renaissance, a young woman named Akemi is murdered. Her body is found in a secluded area near her home. The only clue to her death is a mysterious black kimono that was found near her body. Akemi's husband, Matsumoto, is a samurai warrior who is away on business when she is killed. He returns home to find his wife's body and is devastated. He vows to find her killer and bring them to justice. With the help of his friend and fellow samurai, Yasunari, they set out to find the killer. They soon discover that Akemi was involved in a secret affair with a man named Reiko. Matsumoto is conflicted with his desire for revenge and his honor as a samurai. In the end, he comes to terms with his wife's death and finds closure.
