In the early 1920s, America is in the grip of a crime wave. A series of brutal murders have rocked the nation, and the police are at a loss to find the culprits. Into this atmosphere of fear and violence comes a new detective, with a remarkable gift for solving crimes. This detective is the latest in a long line of supernatural detectives, and he is the only one who can solve this case.

The detective quickly realizes that the murders are the work of a serial killer, and that the killer is targeting specific victims. The detective is able to track the killer to his lair, and he soon faces off against him in a final showdown. The detective is able to defeat the killer and bring him to justice.
