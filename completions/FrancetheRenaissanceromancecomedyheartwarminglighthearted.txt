When Charles, a down-on-his-luck nobleman, learns that he has inherited a small estate in the French countryside, he decides to move there and start anew. He meets his neighbor, a beautiful young woman named Isabelle, and they quickly fall in love. However, they each have a secret that could tear them apart: Isabelle is secretly a peasant, and Charles is secretly penniless.

As they get to know each other, they must grapple with these secrets, as well as the disapproval of their families and the obstacles of their different social classes. But their love is true, and they are determined to find a way to be together. With a little bit of luck and a lot of love, they just might make it work.
