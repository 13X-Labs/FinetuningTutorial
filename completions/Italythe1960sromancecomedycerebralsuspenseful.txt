A young woman, Sophia, falls in love with a man, Roberto, while on vacation in Italy. They have a whirlwind romance and decide to get married. However, Sophia's father objects to the marriage and sends her back to America. Sophia is heartbroken and returns to Italy, hoping to find Roberto again. She eventually learns that he has died. Sophia is devastated and returns home. her father tells her that she must forget Roberto and move on with her life. Sophia does marry another man, but she always feels like she is living in Roberto's shadow.
