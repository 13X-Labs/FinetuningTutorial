In South Africa during the Renaissance, a young woman named Zanele falls in love with a man named Leo. Despite their different backgrounds, the two are drawn to each other and begin a steamy affair. However, they are soon forced to confront the realities of their different social worlds when Leo is married off to a wealthy woman. Zanele is heartbroken, but she soon meets a new man, Thomas, who is also from a different social class. The two of them are drawn to each other and begin their own forbidden love affair. But when Leo's wife is killed, he blames Thomas and vows to destroy him. The two men must fight for the love of their lives, as Leo tries to ruin Thomas's reputation and drive him away. Meanwhile, Zanele is caught in the middle, torn between the two men she loves. Who will she choose?
