It's the 1920s in America and the Great War has just ended. Catherine is a young woman who has just lost her father in the war. She is struggling to make ends meet and is working as a waitress in a speakeasy. One night, she meets a handsome stranger named Harry. They instantly hit it off and begin dating. However, Catherine is not aware that Harry is a bootlegger. When she finds out, she is initially upset but then decides to help him with his business. They begin smuggling alcohol together and eventually fall in love. However, their relationship is put to the test when Harry is arrested and sentenced to jail. Catherine visits him regularly and they continue to plan for their future together. Finally, Harry is released from jail and they are able to start their life together.
