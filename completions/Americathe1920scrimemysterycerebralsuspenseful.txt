The Great Gatsby meets L.A. Confidential in this 1920s crime and mystery film set in America.

The story follows Jay Gatsby, a young and wealthy businessman who moves to Los Angeles to start a new life. He quickly becomes embroiled in the city's criminal underworld, and falls in love with the beautiful and enigmatic Daisy Buchanan.

As Jay tries to win Daisy's heart, he must navigate the dangerous waters of the Los Angeles criminal underworld, where corruption and violence reign. Can Jay find happiness and love in a city full of danger and deceit?

The film is a suspenseful and thrilling ride, full of twists and turns, that will keep audiences guessing until the very end.
