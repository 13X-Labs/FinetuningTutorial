After the death of her mother, Claire is sent to live with her aunt in a small town in France. There, she meets a handsome young man named Jean-Luc. They instantly fall in love, but their relationship is put to the test when they are both drafted into the army to fight in the Algerian War.

As the war wages on, Claire and Jean-Luc must find ways to stay connected and keep their love alive. But when Jean-Luc is captured by the enemy, Claire begins to lose hope that he will ever come home.

With the help of her friends and family, Claire starts to piece her life back together. And when Jean-Luc finally returns home, they must find a way to rebuild their relationship and their lives.
