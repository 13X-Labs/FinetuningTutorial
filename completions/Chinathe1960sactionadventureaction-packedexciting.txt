In the 1960s, China is a country in turmoil. The people are rising up against the government, and the country is on the brink of civil war. Into this chaos comes a young woman named Mei. Mei is a skilled martial artist, and she has come to China to find her missing sister. While searching for her sister, Mei becomes involved in the rebellion against the government. She quickly becomes one of the rebel leaders, and she is determined to overthrow the government and bring freedom to the people of China. But the government is just as determined to crush the rebellion, and the two sides will fight a brutal and bloody battle to the death.
