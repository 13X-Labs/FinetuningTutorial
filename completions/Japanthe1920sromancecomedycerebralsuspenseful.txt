Naomi and Takeo are two young people in 1920s Japan who are madly in love with each other. However, their families are on opposite sides of the country and they are unable to be together. Takeo is a young man from a wealthy family who is being groomed to take over the family business. Naomi is a poor young woman whose family is struggling to make ends meet.

One day, Takeo's family receives an invitation to a party at the home of Naomi's family. Takeo is thrilled at the prospect of finally being able to see Naomi again. However, when he arrives at the party, he is shocked to find that Naomi has been arranged to marry another man.

Takeo is heartbroken, but he knows that he cannot compete with Naomi's wealthy suitor. He leaves the party and returns home, vowing to forget about Naomi.

However, fate intervenes and the two young lovers are brought back together. Takeo's family business is in trouble and he is sent to Naomi's village to help save it. Naomi is thrilled to see Takeo again, but she is still betrothed to another man.

As Takeo and Naomi work together to save the family business, they begin to fall in love all over again. However, Naomi's family is still opposed to their relationship and they will have to fight for their happy ending.
