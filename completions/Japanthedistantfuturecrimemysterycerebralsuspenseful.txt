A young woman, Yumiko, living in a high-tech society in Japan, is haunted by nightmares of a world that Ended. When her dreams become reality and the world around her begins to unravel, she must use her wits to survive and uncover the truth of what is happening.

With the help of her friend, a street-smart teenager named Makoto, Yumiko discovers that the world she knows is a lie. The two of them must race against the clock to uncover the secrets of the government and find out the true nature of the world they live in.

 full of dangerous twists and turns, the two of them must use all their skills to survive as they uncover the darker side of the world they live in.
