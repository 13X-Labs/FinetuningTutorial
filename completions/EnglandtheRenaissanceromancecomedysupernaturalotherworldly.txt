In the midst of the Renaissance in England, two lovers, Rose and Henry, are enamored with each other. However, their love is forbidden by Rose's father, who has arranged for her to marry a wealthy Duke. One night, while Rose is lamenting her fate, she is visited by a fairy godmother who tells her that she will be able to break the curse that has been placed on her. With the help of her fairy godmother, Rose is able to elude her father and the Duke, and she and Henry run away together. They live happily ever after, and the curse is broken.
