When Valerio, a young man from a small Italian town, sets out to find his fortune in the big city, he never expects to get caught up in a world of crime and murder. But that's exactly what happens when he meets Clara, a beautiful and dangerous woman who draws him into a web of intrigue and violence. As the two of them race across Italy in a desperate attempt to stay one step ahead of the law, they find themselves caught up in a deadly game of cat and mouse with an unstoppable enemy. With non-stop action, twists and turns, this heart-pounding thriller will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very end.
