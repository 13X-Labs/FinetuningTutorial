In a small town in France, a young girl named Sophie is taken away by a group of soldiers. She is brought to a castle where she meets the king, who tells her that she is the only one who can save the kingdom from a terrible curse. He gives her a magical book and tells her to find a wizard who can help her break the curse.

Sophie sets out on her quest, making new friends and learning about the world along the way. She eventually finds the wizard, who helps her break the curse. The kingdom is saved and everyone lives happily ever after.
