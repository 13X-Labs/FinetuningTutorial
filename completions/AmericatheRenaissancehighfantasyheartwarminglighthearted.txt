When 12-year-old orphan Anne Shirley is mistakenly sent to live with wealthy cousins in Avonlea, Prince Edward Island instead of the adoptive parents she was expecting, she must learn to navigate her new life in a very different world. While the Cuthberts are initially strict and unkind to Anne, they gradually come to love her as she brings happiness and adventure into their lives. Anne falls in love with the island and its people, making lifelong friends along the way. Even when she faces challenges, like being teased for her red hair or getting in trouble for her temper, Anne knows that she belongs in Avonlea and that she is finally home.
