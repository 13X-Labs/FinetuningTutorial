When a series of brutal murders rock a small town in Renaissance America, detective John Smith is called in to solve the case. With no leads and the community in a panic, Smith must put all his detective skills to the test to find the killer before anyone else falls victim.

As he begins his investigation, Smith quickly discovers that these murders are unlike anything he's ever seen before. The victims all have different wounds, and there appears to be no motive. With the help of his assistant, Sarah, Smith begins to piece together the clues and soon realizes that the killer is targeting specific people for specific reasons.

With the killer's next victim already lined up, Smith races against the clock to stop the murders before anyone else is killed. But as he gets closer to the truth, he realizes that the killer is one step ahead of him. With time running out, Smith must put everything on the line to catch the killer before it's too late.
