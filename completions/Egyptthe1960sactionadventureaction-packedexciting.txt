In the 1960s, Egypt is a hotbed of political and cultural activity. American archaeologist Dr. Indiana Jones is recruited by the US government tofind the legendary Ark of the Covenant before it falls into the wrong hands. Along with his friend and fellow archaeologist, Dr. Marcus Brody, Jones sets out on a perilous journey into the desert.

They are not the only ones after the Ark. Navigating through treacherous sandy dunes, booby traps, and ancient temples, they must outwit the Nazis, who are also after the same prize.

In the end, Jones triumphs, and the Ark is returned to its rightful place.
