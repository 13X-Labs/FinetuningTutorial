In 1920s Japan, a young woman named Hatsumi Shimizu is content working as a maid in her small town. However, her life is turned upside down when a string of murders occur in the town, and Hatsumi is the only one who seems to have any clues to the identity of the killer. With the help of her friend, a newspaper reporter, Hatsumi sets out to catch the killer before he can strike again. Along the way, she discovers that the killer is someone closer to her than she ever could have imagined.
