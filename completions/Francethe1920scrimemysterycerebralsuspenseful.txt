In the early 1920s, a young woman is found brutally murdered in a small town in France. The town's residents are shocked and terrified by the crime, and the police are baffled. One of the victim's closest friends, a woman named Juliette, is determined to find out who did it. She begins her own investigation, and soon discovers that the town's residents are hiding many dark secrets.

As Juliette digs deeper, she realizes that the murder is connected to a string of other unsolved crimes. She eventually discovers that the town's residents are all members of a secret society that has been sacrificing people to a dark god for years. Juliette is able to unmask the killer and expose the society, but not before more innocent people are killed. In the end, justice is served, but the town is left forever tainted by the evil that was done there.
