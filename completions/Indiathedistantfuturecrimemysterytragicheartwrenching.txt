In a future India, a young woman named Ayesha is haunted by the brutal murder of her family. A police detective named Sameer is assigned to the case, and Ayesha quickly falls in love with him. However, as the investigation progresses, it becomes clear that Sameer is not who he seems to be. Ayesha is devastated when she learns that Sameer is actually the killer, and she is forced to watch as he is sentenced to death.
