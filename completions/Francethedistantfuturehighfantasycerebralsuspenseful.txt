In a distant future, the last humans on Earth are living in a city in France. They are constantly under attack by a race of aliens called the Denebs. The Denebs are able to take over the humans' bodies and use them to attack the city. The humans are able to fight back, but they are outnumbered and outgunned. The humans must find a way to defeat the Denebs before they are completely annihilated.
