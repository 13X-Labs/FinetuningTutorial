When Jody Sawyer is dumped by her long-time boyfriend, she turns to her best friend for comfort. Little does she know that her friend has been harbouring a secret crush on her for years. When Jody finally realizes her friend's feelings, she begins to see him in a whole new light.

As their friendship starts to blossom into something more, the two must contend with the disapproval of their families and society. But when Jody's ex-boyfriend comes back into the picture, she must decide who her heart truly belongs to.

Set against the backdrop of the turbulent 1960s, this heart-warming story of friendship, love, and self-discovery is sure to delight audiences of all ages.
