In America, the distant future, a high fantasy film set in a magical land of elves and humans called Narnia. The evil Queen of Narnia, Jadis, has been vanquished and the great lion, Aslan, has been resurrected. The four children of the Pevensie family - Peter, Susan, Edmund and Lucy - are transported back to Narnia where they reign as its rightful rulers. All is well in Narnia until the arrival of the White Witch, who intends to take over the land and enslave its inhabitants. The children, with the help of Aslan and the magical creatures of Narnia, must band together to defeat the White Witch and restore peace and order to Narnia.
