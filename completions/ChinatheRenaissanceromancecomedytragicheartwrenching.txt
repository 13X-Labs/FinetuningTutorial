When the Emperor of China sends his young daughter to marry a lord in a neighboring province, she falls in love with a humble servant boy. Despite the disapproving looks of her new family, the two continue to meet in secret and their love deepens.

When the servant boy is suddenly drafted into the army and sent to war, the young girl is heartbroken. Desperate to be reunited with her true love, she disguises herself as a boy and flees to the battlefield.

After a long and brutal battle, in which many lives are lost, the servant boy is injured and the young girl reveals her true identity. Together, they return home and finally marry. Though their life together is not easy, they are content knowing that they will always have each other.
