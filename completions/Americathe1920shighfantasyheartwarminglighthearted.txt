In the early 1920s, America is a bustling and prosperous country. However, there is a dark force lurking in the shadows, ready to strike at the heart of the country. A group of brave heroes, led by a young woman named Sarah, must join forces to stop the dark force and save America.

The story begins with Sarah, a young woman who lives in a small town in the Midwest. She has a gift for seeing the future, and she is called upon by a group of powerful people to help save America from a dark force that is threatening the country.

Sarah and her allies travel to different parts of America, using their gifts to help people and fight the dark force. They eventually discover that the dark force is actually a group of powerful wizards who are using their magic to control the country.

Sarah and her allies confront the wizards and fight them, eventually defeating them and saving America.
