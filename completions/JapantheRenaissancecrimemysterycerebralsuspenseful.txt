When Setsuna, a young woman in 16th century Japan, unexpectedly inherits her father's criminal empire, she must navigate the dangerous world of samurai politics and intrigue to keep her grip on power. With the help of her loyal bodyguard, she must fend off rivals who would see her dead, all while trying to solve the murders of her father and mother. As she gets closer to the truth, she realizes that the key to her survival lies in uncovering the dark secrets of her family's past.
