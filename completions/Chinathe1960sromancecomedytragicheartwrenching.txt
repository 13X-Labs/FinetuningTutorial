The film opens in China during the 1960s. We are introduced to the main character, Li Mei, who is a young woman living in a small village. Li Mei is in love with a man named Feng, but her parents do not approve of him and want her to marry someone else.

Li Mei is torn between her love for Feng and her duty to her family. She decides to run away with Feng, but her parents find out and try to stop her. Li Mei and Feng are able to get away, but they are pursued by Li Mei's parents and the village elders.

Li Mei and Feng eventually find themselves at the edge of a cliff, with nowhere to go. They share a final embrace before Feng decides to jump off the cliff, taking Li Mei with him. They die in each other's arms, their love stronger than anything else.
