When school girl Haruka Mizuki is transferred to a new school in Kyoto, she had no idea what she was getting herself into. On her first day, she meets the handsome and popular Rei Kagami. Despite their difference in social status, the two quickly become friends.

However, Haruka soon discovers that Rei is betrothed to another woman, a woman he does not love. Haruka is determined to help Rei find true love, even if it means going against tradition. With the help of her friend, the mischievous Yamato Nakamura, the three of them set out to find Rei the perfect match.

But when Haruka begins to fall for Rei herself, she has to decide whether to follow her heart or do what is best for her friend.
