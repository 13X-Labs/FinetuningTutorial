In a distant future, the world is a peaceful and prosperous place. The only thing that seems to be a threat is the occasional meteor shower. One day, a young woman named Mei is out shopping with her friends when a meteor shower strikes. She is hit by a stray meteorite and starts to feel strange. As she starts to experience strange visions, she falls in love with a man she has never met before.

 Mei soon discovers that she has the ability to travel through time. She decides to use her power to go back in time and meet the man she loves. However, she soon discovers that he is in danger of dying in a meteor shower. She must use her power to save him and make sure that they have a future together.
