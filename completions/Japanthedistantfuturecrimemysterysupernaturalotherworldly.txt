It is the year 2120 and Japan has become a dystopia. The government is corrupt and the people are living in fear. A new criminal organization has emerged and is using powerful supernatural abilities to terrorize the populace. The only hope for the people is a team of unlikely heroes - a young girl with psychic abilities, a retired police detective, and a former member of the criminal organization. Together, they must use their own supernatural abilities to stop the criminal organization and bring peace back to Japan.
