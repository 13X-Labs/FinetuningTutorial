A young woman, Sarah, moves to England from America with her new husband, John. They settle into a small town in the countryside and begin to build a life together. However, strange things begin to happen around them and Sarah starts to feel like she is being watched. She becomes paranoid and starts to believe that her husband is keeping secrets from her.

One night, Sarah is woken up by a noise downstairs. She gets out of bed and goes to investigate, but there is nobody there. She starts to feel like she is being followed and her paranoia turns into fear. Eventually, she discovers that her husband has been keeping secrets from her and that he is involved in a dark and dangerous cult. The cult is planning to sacrifice her in a ritualistic murder and she must fight for her life to escape.
