Luc and Marie are two young people who meet and fall in love in France during the 1990s. They have a lot in common, but their relationship is not without its challenges. For one thing, Marie is a bit of a free spirit and Luc is more of a homebody. Additionally, Marie is from a wealthy family and Luc is from a working-class background. Despite all of these differences, the two of them manage to find happiness together.

However, their happiness is not to last. A series of events conspire to tear them apart, including a family tragedy and a bitter disagreement. They eventually drift apart and go their separate ways.

But even though they are no longer together, they still think about each other all the time. And eventually, they both realize that they made a mistake in letting each other go. They get back together and live happily ever after.
