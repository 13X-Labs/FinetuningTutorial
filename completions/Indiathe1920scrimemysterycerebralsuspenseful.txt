In India during the 1920s, a crime lord known as The Maharaja terrorizes the country with his vast network of criminal activity. When the Maharaja's son is killed in a brawl, The Maharaja becomes even more ruthless in his quest for power. The only thing standing in his way is the detective known as Vikram, who is determined to solve the murders and put an end to The Maharaja's reign of terror. Vikram must use all of his skills and resources to outwit The Maharaja and his henchmen, and finally bring him to justice.
