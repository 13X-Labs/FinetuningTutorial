Elizabeth Bennett is a strong-willed young woman who has a sharp wit and a sharp tongue. She is also very much in love with the dashing Mr. Darcy. However, their love is forbidden by their families and so they must find a way to be together.

The story is set in America during the Renaissance and Elizabeth and Darcy must find a way to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. The story is full of suspense, romance, and comedy.
