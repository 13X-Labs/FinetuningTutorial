In the 1990s, India is plagued by a supernatural crime wave. hideous monsters and ghosts commit ghastly crimes, and the police are powerless to stop them. Enter our heroes, a team of paranormal investigators who use their knowledge of the occult to battle the forces of evil.

The team is led by their fearless leader, Rakesh, who is a renowned shaman. Together, they use their combined knowledge of the supernatural to track down and destroy the creatures that are terrorizing the people of India.

This is a story of good versus evil, of courage in the face of adversity, and of the triumph of the human spirit.
