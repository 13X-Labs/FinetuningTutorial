In the future, France is a dystopian country that is plagued by crime and violence. The government is corrupt and the people are living in poverty. One day, a group of criminals commit a brutal murder of a family in their home. The only witness to the crime is the family's young daughter, who is now orphaned. The daughter is taken in by the police and is placed in protective custody. The police are baffled by the crime and are unable to solve it. The daughter is the only one who can identify the killers, but she is too scared to speak up. The killers are still at large and the daughter is living in fear. The story is a race against time as the daughter tries to find the courage to speak up before the killers find her.
