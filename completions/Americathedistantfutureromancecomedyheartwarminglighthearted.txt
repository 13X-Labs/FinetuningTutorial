In the distant future, America is a bustling metropolis where love is in the air. Eva and Alex are two young people who are madly in love with each other. However, their families are complete opposites. Eva's family is wealthy and well-known, while Alex's family is struggling to make ends meet.

 Eva and Alex keep their relationship a secret from their families, but eventually they are caught. Eva's family is furious and disowns her, while Alex's family is overjoyed.

With nowhere to go, Eva and Alex move in together. They get married and start a family of their own. They live happily ever after and their love story is celebrated by all.
