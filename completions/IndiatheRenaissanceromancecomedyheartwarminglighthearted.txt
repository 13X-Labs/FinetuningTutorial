India, Renaissance period. Amar, a lowly cane-cutter falls in love with Jassi, a beautiful young woman of a wealthy family. Jassi's father, disapproving of Amar, arranges for her to marry another man. Amar is heartbroken. Jassi's wedding day arrives. Amar, desperate to stop the wedding, kidnaps Jassi and takes her away. Jassi's family sets out to find her. Amar and Jassi, on the run, have many adventures together, during which they fall deeper in love. Eventually they are found by Jassi's family. Amar is forgiven and Jassi's father accepts him. Jassi and Amar are married and live happily ever after.
