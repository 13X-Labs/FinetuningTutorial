In a near-future England, two young lovers, Rose and James, are tragically torn apart when James is drafted into the military and sent to fight in a foreign war. As they struggle to cope with the pain of their separation, they each find comfort in new relationships. But when James is unexpectedly killed in combat, Rose is left devastated. She finds solace in her new boyfriend, but as she begins to move on with her life, she can't help but wonder if she'll ever truly be able to let go of James.
