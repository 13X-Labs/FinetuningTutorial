In the distant future, America is a crime-ridden wasteland. The only way to keep the peace is to hire teams of bounty hunters to track down and bring in the criminals. One such team is led by a strong-willed woman named Sarah. Sarah and her team are the best in the business, and they're about to take on their biggest challenge yet: finding and capturing a notorious criminal named Jack. But as they get closer to their target, they realize that they may not be the only ones after him. They'll have to use all their skills and ingenuity to outwit their rivals and bring Jack to justice.
