When Maude Lin meets Leo Chang at a friend's wedding, there's an instant connection. But their relationship is put to the test when Maude's father, a wealthy businessman, objects to the match and tries to arranged a more advantageous marriage for her. Determined to follow her heart, Maude and Leo must overcome numerous obstacles, including a disapproving mother-in-law, before they can find their happily ever after.

Set against the backdrop of China's Cultural Revolution, Maude and Leo's story is one of forbidden love, family duty, and finding the courage to be true to oneself. With its mix of romance, comedy, and suspense, the film appeals to a wide range of viewers, offering something for everyone.
