It is the 1960s in France and love is in the air! Pierre is a young man who is madly in love with his girlfriend, Nicole. But when Pierre's best friend, Michel, falls in love with Nicole's best friend, Marie, the four friends must navigate their way through the ups and downs of young love.

The story follows the four friends as they experience the joys and heartaches of first love. Pierre and Nicole's relationship is put to the test when Nicole's disapproving parents try to keep them apart. Michel and Marie's relationship is also fraught with challenges, as Marie is determined to stay true to her promise to her dying father to never marry.

Throughout it all, the four friends rely on each other for support and advice. They laugh together, cry together, and ultimately grow up together. By the end of the story, all four are changed by the power of love.
