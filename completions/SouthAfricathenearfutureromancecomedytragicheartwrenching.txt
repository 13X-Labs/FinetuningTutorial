In the near future, in South Africa, a young woman named Sarah falls in love with a man named David. They meet by chance and quickly fall in love. Sarah is a free spirit and loves to travel, while David is more reserved and prefers to stay at home.

However, their different lifestyles begin to clash and their relationship starts to suffer. Sarah starts to feel like she's losing herself in the relationship and eventually decides to end things.

Heartbroken, David tries to move on but finds that he can't. He discovers that Sarah was the love of his life and he's not able to let her go.

In desperation, David decides to travel to Sarah's home in the hopes of winning her back. He arrives just in time to save her from a dangerous situation. Sarah is surprised and touched by David's gesture and they reconcile.

They spend the rest of their lives together happily ever after.
