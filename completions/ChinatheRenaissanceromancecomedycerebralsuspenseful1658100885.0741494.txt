When Marco Polo returns to China after many years away, he finds that much has changed. The once-great empire is now in decline, and the people are restless. However, Marco finds himself drawn to a beautiful young woman, Mei Li, who represents everything that is good about China.

As Marco and Mei Li get to know each other, they fall in love. However, their relationship is not smooth sailing. The court is in turmoil and there are many contenders for the throne. Marco and Mei Li must navigate these dangerous waters if they are to stay together.

The story culminates with a dramatic showdown between Marco and Mei Li's enemies. With the help of Mei Li's martial arts skills, they are able to triumph and finally be together.
