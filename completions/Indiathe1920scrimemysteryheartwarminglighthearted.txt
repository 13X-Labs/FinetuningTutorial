In the 1920s India, crime and mystery are afoot. The intrepid detective Rajani Singh is on the case, determined to solve the crimes and bring the perpetrators to justice. With the help of her trusted sidekick, Inspector Jai, Rajani will stop at nothing to get to the bottom of the mystery.

But it's not all seriousness and detective work; Rajani and Jai also have a lighthearted and heartwarming friendship, and the film is filled with humor and banter. Along the way, they'll face challenges and danger, but they'll always have each other's backs. In the end, justice will be served and the good guys will prevail.
