After the Great War, England is struggling to find its footing. When Lady Rosemary Richfield's family loses their fortune, she is forced to marry a man she does not love to keep them from ruin. 

Devastated, Rosemary runs away from her wedding and finds herself in the arms of Freddie, a dashing and carefree young man. The two quickly fall in love, but their relationship is put to the test when Freddie's past comes back to haunt him. 

As Freddie's secrets threaten to tear them apart, Rosemary must decide whether to stand by the man she loves or return to the life she was born into.

The story culminates in a heartwarming and romantic climax that will leave audiences cheering for the budding young couple.
