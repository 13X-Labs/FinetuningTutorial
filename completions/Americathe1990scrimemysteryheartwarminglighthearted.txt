In the lighthearted crime mystery set in America during the 1990s, two detectives are tasked with solving a string of murders targeting the city's most wealthy and powerful citizens. As the detectives work to piece together the clues, they come to realize that the killer may be closer to them than they think. With the suspects narrowing and the clock ticking down, the detectives must solve the case before the killer strikes again.

In the end, the detectives unmask the killer, who turns out to be the city's mayor. With the mayor's arrest, the city can finally breathe a sigh of relief and return to normal.
