The year is 2200 and the world is a very different place. The great pyramids of Egypt are now a tourist destination for the wealthy and the country is ruled by a monarchy. However, there is one thing that has remained the same: love.

A young woman named Kiya is in love with a man named Ahmed, but her father does not approve of him. Kiya is arranged to marry a man she does not love and she is forced to go through with the wedding. However, on her wedding night, she decides to run away with Ahmed.

Kiya and Ahmed must use all of their wit and cunning to avoid being caught by her father and husband. They must also deal with the obstacles that come with being from two different social classes. But ultimately, their love will triumph and they will find their Happily Ever After.
