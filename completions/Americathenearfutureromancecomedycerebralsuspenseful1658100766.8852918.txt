In the near future, in America, two young people meet and fall in love. Though their relationship is forbidden by their respective families, they can't help but be drawn to each other.

As their love affair deepens, the couple must grapple with the fears and prejudices that threaten to tear them apart. With the help of their friends and families, they fight for their love, and ultimately triumph over the forces that seek to keep them apart.

The film is a romance & comedy that celebrates the power of love to overcome all obstacles.
