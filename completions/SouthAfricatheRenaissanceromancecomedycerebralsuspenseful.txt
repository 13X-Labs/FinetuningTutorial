In the land of South Africa, during the Renaissance, two young lovers, William and Kate, meet and fall in love. However, their families are bitter enemies, and they are soon forced to part ways. William is sent off to study in England, while Kate is left behind, heartbroken.

Years pass, and the two lovers find themselves back in South Africa. However, they are now on opposite sides of a bloody conflict. As the war rages on, they will have to fight for their love, and their lives, against all odds.
