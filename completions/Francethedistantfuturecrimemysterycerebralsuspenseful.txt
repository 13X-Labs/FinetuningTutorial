Samantha Galloway is a private investigator in Paris, France. She's been hired by a wealthy businessman to find his missing daughter, Olivia.

Samantha begins her investigation by interviewing Olivia's friends and family. She quickly discovers that Olivia was involved in a secret relationship with a man named Simon.

Samantha tracks down Simon and questions him about Olivia's disappearance. He claims that he doesn't know where she is, but Samantha doesn't believe him.

She continues to investigate and discovers that Simon is part of a smuggling ring. Olivia was likely killed after she stumbled upon their illegal activities.

Samantha confronts Simon and his associates. A fierce shootout ensues and Samantha is able to take them down. She returns the businessman his daughter's body and brings the smuggling ring to justice.
