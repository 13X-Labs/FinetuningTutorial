Natalie, a high school student in the early 1990s, is transported to a fantastical world that is reminiscent of America during the pioneer days. In this world, she befriends a group of magical creatures who help her on her journey to find her way back home. Along the way, she falls in love with a charming prince who helps her defeat the evil forces that are trying to keep her in this world. With the help of her new friends, Natalie is able to find her way back home and learns that the magic of friendship is stronger than anything else.
