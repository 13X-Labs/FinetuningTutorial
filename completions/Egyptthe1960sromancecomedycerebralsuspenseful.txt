Cairo, 1960. Nour is a young Egyptian woman who has just been married off to a wealthy older man. She is completely unequipped to deal with the demands of her new life, and is soon replacement by her husband's much younger second wife.Humiliated and isolated, Nour turns to a life of crime to get back at her husband and his new wife. She quickly becomes the city's most notorious jewel thief.

Meanwhile, Karim is a young police detective who has been tasked with catching Nour. He's quickly captivated by her and sets out to track her down.

As Nour and Karim circle each other, the tension between them builds, culminating in a final showdown that will change both of their lives forever.
