When Catherine's parents die from the plague, she is left to fend for herself in a small town in South Africa. She is taken in by a kindly couple, who teach her how to survive in the wild. As she grows older, she becomes a beautiful young woman, and attracts the attention of the local lord, Lord Richard. Lord Richard is a kind and fair man, but is betrothed to another woman. However, he cannot help but be drawn to Catherine.

Catherine is also pursued by a young man named Andrew, who is the son of a wealthy merchant. Andrew is smitten with Catherine, and is determined to win her heart.

As the two men vie for her affections, Catherine must decide which path to take. Will she follow her heart, or will she make a pragmatic choice?

The story ends with a heartwarming twist, as Catherine's true love is revealed.
