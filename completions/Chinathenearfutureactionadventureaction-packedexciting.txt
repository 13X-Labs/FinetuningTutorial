In China's near future, the country is in turmoil. The government is corrupt and the people are suffering. A small group of rebels decide to take matters into their own hands and start a revolution. They are quickly joined by others who share their vision of a better China.

The rebel leader is a young woman named Mei. She is a skilled fighter and strategist. She quickly gains the respect of her fellow rebels.

Together, they start to overthrow the corrupt government. They fight bravely against overwhelming odds. In the end, they succeed in toppling the government and establish a new, more just society.
