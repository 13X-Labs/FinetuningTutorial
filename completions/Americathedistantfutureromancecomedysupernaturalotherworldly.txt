In the distant future, America is a supernatural and otherworldly place. The inhabitants of this strange land have developed various powers and abilities, and the world is full of mystery andmagic. One day, a young woman named Sarah discovers that she has the ability to time travel. She meets a handsome man named John, and the two of them instantly fall in love. However, their love is threatened by a dark force that is trying to destroy the world. Sarah and John must travel back in time to save the future and their love.
