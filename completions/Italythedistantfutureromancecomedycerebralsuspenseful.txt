In the future, the world is a far different place. Italy is a beautiful country that is rich in culture and history. The city of Rome is a bustling metropolis filled with people from all walks of life. One day, a young woman named Sofia is walking through the city when she notices a handsome man across the street. She is instantly attracted to him and decides to follow him. She eventually learns that his name is Lorenzo and that he is a famous artist. The two of them begin to spend time together and soon fall in love with each other. However, their relationship is not without its challenges. Lorenzo is a very private person and is often reluctant to let Sofia into his life. Additionally, the two of them come from different backgrounds and have different outlooks on life. Despite all of these challenges, the two of them continue to grow closer to each other and eventually get married. They live happily ever after and have a family of their own.
