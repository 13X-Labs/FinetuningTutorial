In a future England, society has become increasingly controlled and the government has become a totalitarian dictatorship. In response to this, a group of rebels have formed an underground resistance movement in order to overthrow the government and return England to a free society. The rebels are led by a woman named Sarah, who is a skilled fighter and strategist.

The rebels have planned a major attack on the government's main headquarters in London. Sarah and her team of fighters will infiltrate the building and plant explosives in order to destroy it. However, the government has planned for this attack and has set up a trap for the rebels. Sarah and her team are ambushed by government forces and a fierce battle ensues.

During the battle, Sarah is killed. However, her team of rebels are able to escape and continue the fight against the government. They vow to continue Sarah's mission and make England a free country again.
