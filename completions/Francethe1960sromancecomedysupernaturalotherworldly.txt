It's the 1960s in France, and the world is a different place. Supernatural creatures exist and often cross over into the human realm. One day, a young woman named Elise finds herself in the middle of a supernatural conspiracy. She's being pursued by a group of evil beings who want to use her for their own nefarious purposes. Luckily, she's also being protected by a handsome vampire named Olivier. As the two of them try to stay one step ahead of the bad guys, they also find themselves falling in love with each other. But can their love survive the dangers they're facing?
