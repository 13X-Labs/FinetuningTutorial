In the 1920s, young lovebirds Daisy and Dan meet and fall madly in love. Daisy is a wealthy debutante and Dan is a struggling artist. Despite their different backgrounds, the two are drawn to each other and begin a whirlwind romance.

Unfortunately, their happiness is not meant to last. Daisy's family disapproves of Dan and forbids them from seeing each other. Daisy is heartbroken, but she reluctantly obeys her family's wishes.

Dan is determined to win Daisy back, and he finally does. However, just as they are about to be reunited, tragedy strikes. Daisy's family is killed in a car accident, leaving her all alone.

Daisy is devastated, but Dan is there for her. He helps her through her grief and they rekindle their romance. They get married and live happily ever after.
