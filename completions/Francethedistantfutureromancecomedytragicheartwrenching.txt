In the distant future, France is a bustling metropolis where love is in the air. But for one young woman, love is anything but simple.

Claire is a 20-something woman who is desperately in love with a man named Laurent. Laurent is a handsome, successful man who seems to have it all. But he is also married, and Claire is his mistress.

Claire knows that she is risking everything by being with Laurent. If his wife ever found out, she would be devastated. But she can't help herself - she is head-over-heels in love with him.

The affair is passionate and intense, but it is also tragic. Claire knows that she can never have Laurent to herself. He will always belong to his wife. But she can't bear to let him go, and so she continues to love him, even though it hurts.
