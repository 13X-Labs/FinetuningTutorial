During the Renaissance in Japan, two lovers, Oda and Miyuki, must find a way to be together despite the disapproval of their families. As rival clans battle for control of the country, the lovers must keep their relationship a secret or risk being caught in the crossfire.

As the country is torn apart by war, Oda and Miyuki must find a way to be together. They must find a way to love each other in a time of violence and chaos.
