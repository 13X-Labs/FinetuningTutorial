A young woman, Megumi, moves to Tokyo from her small hometown in the countryside to pursue her dream of becoming a singer. She quickly discovers that the city is not as welcoming as she had hoped, and she struggles to find her place. One day, she meets a handsome man, Tatsuya, who helps her out when she's lost. They become fast friends, and Megumi starts to fall for him. However, she soon discovers that Tatsuya is already in a relationship with another woman. Though Megumi is heartbroken, she continues to pursue her dream, and eventually finds success as a singer. Tatsuya remains a good friend, and they continue to support each other through the ups and downs of their lives.
