In a future England that has been taken over by a totalitarian government, a group of rebels are fighting back against the oppressive regime. They are led by a woman named Sarah, who has been inspired by the ideals of the country's former democracy. Sarah and her team of rebels are fighting for freedom and democracy, and they will stop at nothing to achieve their goals. They are up against a government that is armed with the latest in technology and weaponry, but the rebels are not afraid to fight back and they have the support of the people. With the help of the people, they will eventually overthrow the government and restore democracy to England.
