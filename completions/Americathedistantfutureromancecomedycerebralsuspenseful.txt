In the future, America is a divided country. The East is a wealthy and advanced society, while the West is a poor and underdeveloped region. Despite the differences between them, the two regions are connected by a huge wall.

One day, a young woman from the East, Penelope, decides to travel to the West. She is quickly captured by a group of bandits. However, she is rescued by a handsome stranger, Jesse. The two of them quickly fall in love and begin a journey across the country, trying to find a way to the East.

As they travel, they are pursued by the authorities from both the East and the West. They must use all their cunning and intelligence to stay ahead of their pursuers and find a way to safety. Along the way, they discover a shocking secret about the East that will change their lives forever.
