When Valeria, a young woman in Renaissance Italy, is accused of being a witch, she is brought before the tribunal of the Inquisition. Valeria is certain that she will be found guilty and burned at the stake, but she is surprised when the tribunal offers her a deal. If she can find the witch who has been causing problems in the town, they will let her go.

Valeria begins her search, but quickly realizes that this is no ordinary witch. The witch is using powerful magic to stay hidden, and Valeria will need all of her own magical skills to track her down. The chase leads Valeria across the country, and she soon discovers that the witch is after a very powerful magical artifact. Valeria must find the witch before she can get her hands on the artifact, or the consequences will be disastrous.
