In the near future, a group of criminals decide to rob a bank in Italy. They have planned and rehearsed for months, but they are still nervous. As they drive to the bank, they are followed by a police car. The police are tipped off about the robbery and they try to stop the criminals. A high-speed chase ensues and the criminals are finally cornered. They open fire on the police, but the police are ready and they return fire. A gun battle ensues and the criminals are finally captured.
