In a distant future, India is a land of great turmoil and upheaval. Powerful forces are battling for control of the country, and the people are caught in the middle. In the midst of this chaos, a young woman named Pari comes of age. She is a skilled warrior and has the potential to become a great leader. However, she is also a tragic figure, torn between her duty to her country and her love for her family.

Pari is forced to choose between her duty and her love when her country is attacked by a massive army. The army is led by a powerful sorcerer, who has come to take control of India. Pari knows that she must stop him, but doing so will mean betraying her family. She must make a decision, and whatever she chooses will have tragic consequences.
