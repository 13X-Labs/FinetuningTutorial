In a future America, crime is rampant and the justice system is broken. A young woman named Sarah is brutally murdered, and her killers are never brought to justice. Her husband, John, is consumed with grief andrage. He becomes a vigilante, determined to find Sarah's killers and make them pay. John's quest for justice takes him into the dark underworld of the city, where he quickly learns that there is no such thing as justice - only vengeance. Sarah's killers are powerful and dangerous men, and John is quickly outmatched. But he is driven by his love for Sarah and his desire to avenge her, and he will not rest until her killers are brought to justice - even if it costs him his life.
