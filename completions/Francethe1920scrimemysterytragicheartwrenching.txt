In the 1920s, France is in the throes of a crime wave. A young woman, Sophie, is brutally murdered in her apartment. The police are baffled by the case, and the press is speculating that a serial killer is on the loose. Sophie's boyfriend, Pierre, is devastated by her death. He was supposed to meet her that night, but he was delayed by work. Pierre is consumed with guilt and decides to take matters into his own hands. He begins to investigate the case himself, and he quickly discovers that Sophie was involved in a secret relationship with a married man. Pierre is determined to find the killer and bring him to justice. However, the more he digs, the more he realizes that the killer is someone who is very close to him.
