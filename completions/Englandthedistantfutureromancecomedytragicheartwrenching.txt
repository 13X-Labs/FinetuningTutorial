On a cold winter's night in England, a young woman named Catherine is alone in her house, reading a book by the fire. She hears a knock at the door and she opens it to find a handsome stranger named Edward. He tells her that he is a time traveler from the future, and he has come to take her with him. She is hesitant at first, but she decides to go with him.

They travel to Edward's time, which is a dystopian future where the world is ruled by a tyrannical government. The two of them fall in love and start a rebel group to overthrow the government. After many dangerous adventures, they are finally successful in overthrowing the government and they live happily ever after.
