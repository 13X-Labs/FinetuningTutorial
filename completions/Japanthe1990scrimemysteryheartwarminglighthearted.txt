In thecrime & mystery film set in Japan during the 1990s, a young woman named Sakura is trying to solve a series of murders that have been happening in her city. She teams up with a detective named Akiko to try and solve the case. However, Sakura is also dealing with her own personal issues, as she is trying to find out who her real father is. Throughout the film, the two women bond over their love of justice and solve the murders together.
