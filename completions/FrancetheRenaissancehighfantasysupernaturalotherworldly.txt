When Valerian and Laureline, two special operatives of the autonomous human organization known as the Combined Regulatory Agency of Uninhabited Sectors, are on a routine mission to keep order in the human territories, they are suddenly thrown into a brewing conflict between the different orders of magic. Valerian and Laureline must now prevent an all-out magical war that would devastate the human world.

As they uncover the true nature of the conflict, they must form unlikely alliances with some of the rival factions in order to bring about a peaceful resolution. But the stakes are high, and the cost of failure could be the extermination of all magic and those who practice it.

Valerian and Laureline must use all of their skills to navigate the complex political landscape of the Renaissance, and find a way to end the conflict before it destroys everything they hold dear.
