In the near future, South Africa is in the midst of a civil war. The government is corrupt and the people are suffering. A group of freedom fighters has emerged, calling themselves the Resistance. They are fighting for the liberation of their people.

The story follows a young woman named Lila, who joins the Resistance. She is taken under the wing of the group's leader, a man named Gabriel. Lila is determined to make a difference and fight for the freedom of her people.

The Resistance is up against a formidable enemy, and they are outnumbered and outgunned. But they are fighting for a cause they believe in, and they are willing to risk everything for it.

The story is full of action and adventure, as the Resistance battles the enemy in a desperate attempt to win freedom for their people.
