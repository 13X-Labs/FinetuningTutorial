In France during the Renaissance, a crime & mystery film set in a heartwarming & lighthearted story. A group of friends are gathered together in a small town for a wedding. However, the night before the wedding, one of the friends is found dead in a suspicious circumstances. The group decides to investigate the death and uncover a conspiracy involving the local nobility. Along the way, they discover that the dead friend was actually part of a much larger conspiracy to overthrow the government. With the help of the dead friend's ghost, they are able to thwart the plot and save the day.
