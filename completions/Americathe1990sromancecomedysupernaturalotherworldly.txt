WhenPenelope and her friends stumble upon a mysterious old house deep in the woods, they never expected to find the key to another world. But that's exactly what they get when they enter the portal to the fairy-tale realm.

Now, in a land of enchantment and magic, Penelope must find her true love before it's too late. With the help of her friends - and a few surprises along the way - she just might find her Happily Ever After.
