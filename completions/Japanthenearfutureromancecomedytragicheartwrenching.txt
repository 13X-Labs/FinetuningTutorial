In the future, Japan is a country that is on the verge of collapse. The government is corrupt and the economy is in shambles. The people are living in poverty and crime is rampant. One day, a young woman named Sakura is walking home from work when she is attacked by a gang of thugs. She is saved by a mysterious man named Kaito who tells her that she is special and that he has been sent to protect her.

Sakura and Kaito quickly fall in love, but they live in a world where love is forbidden. They must keep their relationship a secret or risk being arrested. As the two try to navigate their relationship in a world that is against them, they must also deal with the problems that are plaguing Japan. Can their love survive in a world that is falling apart?
