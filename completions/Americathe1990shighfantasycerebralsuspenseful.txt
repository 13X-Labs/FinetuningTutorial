In a small town in 1990s America, an awkward teenager discovers she has latent magical powers. With the help of her new friends – a rebellious girl and a shy boy – she learns to control her powers and discovers a hidden world of magic that exists alongside the everyday world.

The three friends quickly get in over their heads when they uncover a dark plot by a group of magic users who are planning to use their powers to control the town. The teens must stop them before it's too late.

Full of heart and adventure, the film follows the three friends as they battle evil forces, discover their own strengths and weaknesses, and come to understand the true power of magic.
