In the future, India is a bustling metropolis where magic and technology have converged. Despite the advances, the city is still plagued by poverty and crime. When a young girl named Leela is orphaned, she is taken in by a kind-hearted woman named Devi. Leela quickly learns that Devi is a witch with the power to grant wishes. Leela wishes to help those in need, so Devi teaches her how to use her magic to do good. Together, they help those who are less fortunate and make the city a better place. Along the way, they make new friends and face challenges, but ultimately their friendship and magic make the city a better place for all.
