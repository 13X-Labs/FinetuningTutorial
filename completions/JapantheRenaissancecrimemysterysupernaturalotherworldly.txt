In Japan during the Renaissance, a crime and mystery film is set in motion when a group of samurai are hired to escort a young noblewoman to a remote temple. Along the way, they are ambushed by a group of ninjas who are after the woman. The samurai are able to protect her and they safely reach the temple. 

Inside the temple, they find that the woman is not only being possessed by a demon, but she is also the key to a portal that leads to another world. The evil spirits that are trying to enter our world are using her as a vessel. With the help of the priests at the temple, the samurai are able to fight off the demons and Close the portal. The woman is then exorcised and the film ends with the samurai returning her safely to her home.
