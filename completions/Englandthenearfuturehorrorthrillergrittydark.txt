The film is set in England in the near future. The country is in the grip of a virulent outbreak of a new disease which is highly contagious and affects the mind, body and soul of its victims. The government has imposed a strict curfew in an attempt to contain the spread of the disease, but it is proving to be increasingly difficult as the outbreaks continue to occur.

The film follows a group of survivors who are fighting to stay alive in the face of this apocalyptic nightmare. They are forced to barricade themselves inside a small house in the countryside, where they are constantly besieged by hordes of the infected. The group must use all their ingenuity and courage to find a way out of this nightmare before it consumes them completely.
