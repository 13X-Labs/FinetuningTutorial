In the future, America is a desolate wasteland. The only people left are the rich and powerful. In the midst of this chaos, a young woman named Sarah falls in love with a wealthy man named John. Despite the odds, they manage to find happiness together. However, their happiness is short-lived when John is killed by a rival gang. Sarah is devastated, but she is determined to get revenge. With the help of her friends, she sets out to take down the rival gang and bring justice to John's memory. Along the way, Sarah discovers that love can still conquer all, even in the darkest of times.
