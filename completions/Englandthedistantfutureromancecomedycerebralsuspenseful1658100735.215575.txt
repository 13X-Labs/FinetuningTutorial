In the dystopian future of England, the government has implemented a new law that all citizens must be paired with a life partner by the time they turn 25. If they are not, they are sent to prison. At the age of 25, our heroine, Sophie, is still single and about to be sent to prison. She hatches a plan to escape by finding a fake husband. She enlists the help of her best friend, Emma, to find someone who can pose as her husband. They settle on a young man named Alex. Alex is a struggling musician who is down on his luck. He agrees to pose as Sophie's husband for a fee. The plan seems to be working until Sophie falls in love with Alex for real. Now she must choose between the man she loves and the life she wants.
