India, a land of love and pollution. The year is 3012 and the world is a mess. The wealthy live in opulent compounds far above the smog, while the poor toil away in the filth below. One day, a young woman named Kala is walking home from work when she sees a man being chased by a mob. She rescues him and falls in love with him, only to discover that he is a prince from a rival kingdom. The two of them must overcome religious differences, political turmoil, and their own personal demons in order to be together.
